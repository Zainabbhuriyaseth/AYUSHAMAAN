Advanced imaging techniques for accurate and detailed diagnosis.

The Role of Radiology in Healthcare

Radiology is a medical specialty that uses imaging to diagnose and treat diseases seen within the body. Radiologists are expert medical doctors who interpret these images to provide crucial information for your healthcare. At AYUSHMAAN, our radiology department is equipped with cutting-edge technology to perform a wide array of diagnostic imaging procedures, ensuring your doctor receives a clear and precise picture of your health.

Our Core Radiology Services

  • check_circleDigital X-Ray: Provides fast and clear images of bones and certain soft tissues, essential for diagnosing fractures, infections, and other conditions.
  • check_circleUltrasound (USG): Uses sound waves to create real-time images of organs and blood vessels. It is a safe, radiation-free method for various examinations, including obstetrics.
  • check_circleColor Doppler: A specialized type of ultrasound that allows us to visualize blood flow through arteries and veins, critical for detecting blockages or clots.
  • check_circleMammography: A specific type of breast imaging that uses low-dose x-rays to detect cancer early, before women experience symptoms.
  • check_circleOPG (Orthopantomogram): A panoramic dental X-ray of the upper and lower jaws, providing a complete overview of the teeth and jaw structure.

Patient-Centered Imaging Experience

We are committed to making your imaging procedure as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

  1. Consultation & Preparation: Our team will explain the procedure clearly and provide any necessary preparation instructions. We encourage you to ask any questions you may have.
  2. The Procedure: Our skilled technologists will position you correctly and guide you through each step of the imaging process, ensuring your comfort and safety at all times.
  3. Expert Interpretation: After your scan, our team of experienced radiologists will meticulously analyze the images and compile a comprehensive report for your referring physician.
