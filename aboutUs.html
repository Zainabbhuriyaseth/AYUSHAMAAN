About AAYUSHMAAN Diagnostics

A Legacy of Trust, A Future of Excellence in Healthcare

Our Story

Founded with the vision to bring world-class diagnostic services to the heart of Surat, AAYUSHMAAN MRI & Diagnostics Centre began its journey as a single, dedicated facility. Our goal was simple yet profound: to provide every patient with precise, reliable, and timely diagnostic insights in a compassionate and comfortable environment.

Over the years, we have grown into a leading name in healthcare, trusted by doctors and patients alike. Our commitment has always been to invest in the latest technology and the finest medical expertise to ensure that our community has access to the best care possible.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled diagnostic accuracy and patient care through advanced technology, ethical practices, and a dedicated team of professionals, ensuring the well-being of our community.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected diagnostic healthcare provider in the region, renowned for our clinical excellence, technological superiority, and unwavering commitment to patient health.

State-of-the-Art Facility

Our center is designed with your comfort and safety in mind. From our spacious, clean waiting areas to our advanced diagnostic rooms, every detail has been carefully considered. We are proud to house some of the most sophisticated equipment in the industry, including a 3T MRI and a 160 Slice CT Scanner, which allow for faster, clearer, and more detailed imaging.
